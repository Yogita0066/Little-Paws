Jack Russell Terrier

The Jack Russell Terrier is a British breed of small terrier. It is principally white-bodied and smooth-, rough- or broken-coated, and can be any colour.

Colors:
predominantly white, with patches of tan or black
Life expectancy:
13 – 16 years
Temperament:
Clownish, Intelligent, Vocal, Athletic, Energetic, Stubborn, Fearless
Weight: 6 – 8 kg (Adult)
Height: 25–30 cm (10–12 in)
