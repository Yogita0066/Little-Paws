Sarabi dog or Persian mastiff

The Sarabi dog or Persian mastiff is a large breed of livestock guardian dog from Iran, originating from the Sarab County, Sarabi dogs have been used for centuries by local shepherds to protect herds of sheep and goats from bears, wolves, jackals and other local predators.

Color:
Black, Red
Life expectancy:
10 - 12 Years
Temperament:
Loyal, Obedient, Playful
Weight:
Male: 50 - 55 kg
Female: 45 - 50 kg
